# Title

## How to Write Effective Title for SEO
### What is a Title Tag?
The title tag defines the title of a web page. It appears in search engine results and browser tabs.

### Why It Matters

- **SEO**: Helps search engines understand and rank your page.
- **User Experience**: Provides a preview of the page content.
- **CTR**: Influences the likelihood of users clicking on your link.

### Best Practices

1. **Incorporate Keywords**: Use relevant keywords naturally.
2. **Keep It Concise**: Aim for 10-30 characters to avoid truncation.
3. **Be Unique**: Ensure each page has a distinct title.
4. **Include Branding**: Add your brand name, ideally at the end.
5. **Use Action Words**: Engage users with actionable language.
6. **Match User Intent**: Align with what users are searching for.
7. **Avoid Keyword Stuffing**: Use keywords sparingly and naturally.
8. **Regularly Review**: Monitor and adjust based on performance.

### Common Mistakes

- Generic titles like “Home” or “Welcome.”
- Overly long titles that get cut off.
- Duplicate titles across multiple pages.
